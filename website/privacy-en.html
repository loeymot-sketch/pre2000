Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy is our priority. This policy describes how the "Mama & Bébé" app, published by KSA MEDIA LLC, collects, uses and protects your personal data.

2. Data Collected

  • Account Information: Email address (only for authentication via Firebase Auth).
  • Health Data: Pregnancy-related information (pregnancy week, weight, medical appointments) that you enter manually. This data is stored securely on Google Firebase.
  • Technical Data: Anonymous technical identifiers to ensure proper app functioning and detect errors (via Sentry).

3. Use of Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide you with pregnancy tracking features.
  • Sync your data across your devices (if you are logged in).
  • Improve app stability (anonymous crash detection via Sentry).

We do not sell, rent or share any personal data with third parties for commercial or advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following secure services:

  • Google Firebase: Secure data hosting and authentication. Firebase Policy.
  • Sentry: Anonymous technical error detection. No personal data is transmitted. Sentry Policy.

5. Security

All communications between the app and our servers are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Your data is stored on Google Cloud Platform's secure infrastructure (Firebase).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify and delete your data at any time:

  • Delete in-app: Go to Profile → Settings → Delete my account. All your data will be immediately and permanently erased.
  • By email: Contact us at contact@mama-et-bebe.com for any data-related request.

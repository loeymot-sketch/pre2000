Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I create an account?

When launching the app, click "Create an account". Enter your email address and a secure password. You can also use the app in guest mode (without an account).

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app, go to Profile → Settings → Delete my account. All your data will be immediately and permanently erased from our servers. You can also email us at contact@mama-et-bebe.com.

I forgot my password

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password". Enter your email and you will receive a reset link within seconds.

Is the app free?

Yes, Mama & Bébé is 100% free, with no ads and no in-app purchases. All features are accessible from download.

What languages is the app available in?

The app is available in French, English and Arabic. You can change the language at any time in the app settings.

Does the app replace my doctor?

No. Mama & Bébé is a support and general information tool. It does not constitute medical advice. Always consult a healthcare professional for your medical questions.
